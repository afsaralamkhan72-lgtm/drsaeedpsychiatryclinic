Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) Treatment in Peshawar

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) is a structured, evidence-based talk therapy that helps identify and change unhelpful thinking and behavior patterns. Dr. Muhammad Saeed Khan, who holds both a Diploma and Certificate in CBT, offers CBT in Peshawar for a wide range of conditions.

Common Symptoms

  • Anxiety, depression, OCD or related conditions

Our Approach to Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) Treatment

CBT sessions are goal-oriented and structured, typically over a defined number of weeks, teaching practical skills patients can continue using independently after treatment ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many CBT sessions are typically needed?

CBT is usually structured over 6-20 sessions depending on the condition and its severity, with clear goals set at the start of treatment.

Is cognitive behavioral therapy (cbt) consultation with Dr. Muhammad Saeed Khan confidential?

Yes. All consultations for cognitive behavioral therapy (cbt) are strictly confidential, in line with medical ethics and PMDC guidelines. Your privacy is protected whether you consult in person at Hayatabad or online.
